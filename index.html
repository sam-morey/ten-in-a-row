The Venue

A residential backyard. The hoop is toy-sized — the kind typically marketed to children between the ages of three and six. It is installed at a fixed height. No adjustments have been made. The surface is irregular. Conditions vary.

The Format

One official attempt per day. The attempt is filmed. The film is shared. The catchphrase is delivered deadpan, every time, without deviation. There are no re-takes — except for the bonus sessions, which are unofficial and not counted.

The Heartbreak

On two separate occasions, the athlete reached a streak of six consecutive makes — the longest run in the official record. On both occasions, the very next shot was a miss. The sequence has repeated exactly. The record stands at six.

The Mathematics

At the current make rate, the probability of converting ten consecutive shots is approximately 3%. This number is not discouraging. It is simply the number. The campaign continues.
